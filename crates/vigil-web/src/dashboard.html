Workflow

From ingestion to operator action in one coherent flow.

1

Ingest

Collect machine telemetry, maintenance activity, and operator notes with no assumption that sources arrive clean or on time.

2

Detect

Correlate events across time windows and rules so the UI presents incidents, not raw noise.

3

Explain

Show what fired, why it fired, and what evidence raised or lowered confidence.

4

Act

Capture acknowledgment, assignment, reroute, override, or resolution inside the same surface.

5

Replay

Reconstruct the complete decision chain, including reasoning, operator response, and integrity verification.

Design thesis
High-stakes operators do not need more telemetry. They need clarity, confidence, and a defensible path to action.
Vigil keeps incidents dominant, reasoning legible, and auditability first-class. The product is not just about detection. It is about trusted action under uncertainty.
