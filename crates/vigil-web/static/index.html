Capabilities

A decision system,
not a dashboard.

Vigil compresses evidence, reasoning, and operator action into a single surface — so teams move from signal to decision without digging through disconnected tooling.

Explainable Incidents

Every incident carries a rule-firing timeline, suspected cause, and confidence score. No black-box anomalies — only traceable reasoning grounded in real telemetry data.

Workflow

From ingestion
to action in one flow.

Every step is traceable. Every decision is recorded. Every replay is verifiable.

1

Ingest

Collect machine telemetry, maintenance tickets, and operator notes with no assumption that sources arrive clean or on time.

2

Detect

Correlate events across time windows and rules so the system surfaces incidents, not raw noise.

3

Explain

Show what fired, why it fired, and what evidence raised or lowered confidence — grounded in real data.

4

Act

Capture acknowledgment, assignment, reroute, override, or resolution inside the same surface.

5

Replay

Reconstruct the complete decision chain — reasoning, operator response, and Merkle-backed integrity verification.

Design Thesis
"High-stakes operators do not need more telemetry. They need clarity, confidence, and a defensible path to action."

Vigil keeps incidents dominant, reasoning legible, and auditability first-class. The product is not just about detection — it is about trusted action under uncertainty.
